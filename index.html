Bio

Xinhui Li is currently a Master of City Planning Candidate at DUSP, MIT. She is interested in utilizing spatial analytics and data analytics to facilitate design of urban spaces, and use data visualization tools to represent study results and design schemes. Before coming to MIT, Xinhui's research focus included urban green space layout planning considering equitable access, as well as urban residential district design promoting public health. She also worked on landscape architecture projects including stormwater management systems and urban parks. Xinhui holds a Master in Landscape Architecture from Harvard University and a Bachelor of Engineering from Sun Yat-Sen University, China.
